Tableau developer
-----------------

Electrical engineer with a good experience in the field of Tableau Dashboard design. Have also very good knowledge of MS-Excel. Major R&R of my current JOB are following:- 
❖ Collaborating with Business Users/Analysts for requirement gathering process, finalizing the mock up reports and handling clients as a responsible team member.

❖ End-to-end experience in designing and deploying data dashboards/visualizations with various charts in Tableau. 
❖ Applied interactive filters, parameters, LoD’s and calculations for preparing interactive dashboards and worksheets in Tableau. 
❖ Worked with Dashboard actions, context filters, IF statements, CASE statements, date calculations, perform YTD, QTD, WTD and MTD type of analysis. 
❖ Knowledge in various reporting objects like Hierarchies, Filters, calculated fields, Sets, Groups, Parameter, bins etc. in Tableau. 
❖ Experience in interacting with business users and analysts on requirements..
